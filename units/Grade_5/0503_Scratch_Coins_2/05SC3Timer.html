Making a Countdown Timer

You already know everything you need to know about how to make a countdown timer. These are the blocks you'll need to combine to make it - it is up to you to figure it out:

  • Variable for Seconds
  • Forever Block
  • Wait Block
  • Sprites to show the Score, Game Over, and Play Again when the Timer Runs out.
  • Code to reset the timer at the beginning of a new game.
