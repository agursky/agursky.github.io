Sharing your Work

Once you are done with your project, it is time to share it to our shared Scratch Account on the Scratch website. This way other people can play your game or use your app. Once you are in Scratch, look for the Sign in button in the top left corner. The username and password will be on the smartboard. STUDENTS - If Mrs. Manela says she doesn't know the username and password - remind her that I wrote them for her in the lesson plans I left:). To share your work:

  • Sign into our scratch account
  • Click Create
  • Click File - Upload from your computer
  • Find your file and press OK
  • Add your initials to the name
  • Click File - Save Now
