Starting our Joke/Story in Scratch

For the beginning of our joke, we want to have certain pieces in the right place. We need:

  • A background - click on stage and import one to the costumes.
  • A sprite to start telling the joke.
  • A sprite to listen to the joke.
  • A sprite to act out the joke - for example if you are doing why did the chicken cross the road, you'd want to show a chicken crossing a road.

Let's start by getting our background, and our telling and listening sprites onto the stage. Also add the code to have the telling sprite say the beginning of the joke - use the say block in the looks menu.
