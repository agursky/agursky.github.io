What is a Digital Footprint?

Your Digital Footprint affects your life. Things that you post online can be seen by everyone, and what they wsee will affect how they think of you and how they treat you. If you want poeple to treat you with respect, it is important that your digital footprint include words and actions that people respect and admire. In fact, many people today, when they apply for a job, find out that their employer has looked them up on social media. For them, their Digital Footprint may be the what decides whether or not they get the job they want.
