Protecting our Privacy

Look at the words in the table below. Which words do you recognize?

Internet Privacy Vocabulary Words
user/screen name password login malware
logout identity theft spam hack
scam attachment download privacy policy

It is very important to protect our privacy online. There are a lot of people who want to use the computer to get information about us. Some of them want to sell our information, while others want to use our information to steal from us, or do illegal activity using our name. When we protect our private information online, and use the internet in a responsible way, we can make sure this doesn't happen to us. We will learn more by watching this Brainpop video.
