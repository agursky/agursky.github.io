CSS Animations

Animations are like Transitions, except for 1 key differences:

  • They start right away, not when you click or hover on a button.

They are a little harder to do, but with practice, they can be very easy. Here are the steps:

  • In your CSS add two new rules to the thing you want to animate - like a div:
    • animation-name - name it whatever you want.
    • animation-duration - just like in transitions, it should be a number of seconds.
  • Then type @keyframes with your animation name and open brackets inside the CSS rules for the thing you want to animate.
