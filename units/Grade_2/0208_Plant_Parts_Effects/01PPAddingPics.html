Adding Pictures to your Slides

Slideshows are more fun to watch when they have pictures. For this project, we are going to be getting pictures from Picasa. Picasa is a web page that you can use to share your pictures with other people. Here's how you use Picasa to get a picture.

If we want to add a picture to our slides, we have to make room for it first. Right now there isn't a lot of room on the slide. To change that, we have to change the Layout. Layout is where things are. Once you have done that you can drag the picture you want onto the slide.

So to summarize:

  • drag the picture from Picasa onto your Desktop
  • Change the layout of your slide to make room for the picture.
  • Drag the picture from your Desktop onto your slide.
