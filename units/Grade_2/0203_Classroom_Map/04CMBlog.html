Today we will learn review Exporting, and putting our work on our Blog.

Why Do We Export?

When we are done with our work, we want to Export it and put it on our blog. The reason we Export is because a Kid Pix file will not go on our Blog. Our Blogs only know how to have web files. So Exporting makes a copy of your work for the web. Once you have that web file, you are able to put it on your blog. When you export name your work whatever the name was, with an X at the end of it.
