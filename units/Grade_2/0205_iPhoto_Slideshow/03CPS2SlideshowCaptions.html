Adding Captions in iPhoto

Once we've made our Slideshow, and added effects to it, we can also add Captions to the pictures. Captions are words that describe a picture. We are going to add captions to our Computer Parts pictures to show what each of those parts do. It is easy to add a caption to your picture.

We want to write one caption for each computer part. If we have 3 pictures of a mouse we can just write the caption one time and Copy and Paste it on top of the other mouse pictures. That way the people watching the show will see the same caption about a mouse for all of the mouse pictures. We will do that for each computer part.

  • In your Album, click on the picture.
  • Click on the Info Button in the bottom right corner.
  • Click on where it says "Add a Description", and type your caption.

Today's Assignment

Add a caption to each of the pictures in your Slideshow, describing what the computer part helps us do. For Example: for a picture of a keyboard add the Caption "This is the keyboard. It helps us type words into the computer".
