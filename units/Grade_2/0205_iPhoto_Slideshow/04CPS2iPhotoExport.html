Exporting our Slideshow

Just like we Export from Kid Pix to put our work on the blog, we Export from iPhoto. We can Export our iPhoto Slideshow into a video, and then all of our friends can see it. To Export from iPhoto:

  • In you Slideshow, go to File - Export.
  • Choose Slideshow.
  • UNCHECK the checkbox.
  • Press Export and Choose Desktop from the window that pops up.
