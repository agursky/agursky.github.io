Slideshows in iPhoto

It is easy to make a Slideshow in iPhoto. You just have to pick which pictures you want to show, and then press a button. We can make slideshows about anything we want and even choose music and other effects to go with it. To make a slideshow in iPhoto:

  • Drag the pictures you want over to the sidebar to make an Album of them.
  • In the Album drag the pictures into the order you want to show them.
  • Press the Slideshow button on the bottom of the iPhoto Window.
  • When the slideshow plays, press the buttons on the bottom to add effects to it.
