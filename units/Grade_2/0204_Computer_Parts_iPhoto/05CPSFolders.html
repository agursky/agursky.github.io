Making Our Own Folders

It is easy to make your own Folder on the computer. Just click anywhere on the desktop and then go to the top menu. On the otp menu find "File - New Folder". This will make a new folder called "Untitled Folder". You can click on the name of the folder to change it.

This is helpful to us because having Folders on our Desktop helps us keep our Desktop clean. If there are too many pictures on our Desktop, we won't be able to find anything.
