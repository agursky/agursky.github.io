Introduction

Today we are going to add to our Plant Parts Project by using the Text Tool to write an introduction to our show about Plant Parts. An introduction is a good way to get people ready for what they are about to learn. It can be serious, or if you want, it can be funny.

The first thing you need to do is make a new Frame for your Introduction. The way you create a new Frame is by clicking on the "New Blank Frame Button" just like in the picture below.

Things to Remember:

  • Your Introduction slide shouldn't talk about what each plant part does. We will do that later.
  • Your Introduction slide should talk about what your slideshow will be about.
  • Your Introduction should be at least 4 sentences.
  • Sentences should begin with a capital letter.
