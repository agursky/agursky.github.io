Plant Parts Slides

We are now going to make the slides for each of our plant parts. Remember, there are 4 plant parts that we are going to look at: Roots, Stem, Flower, and Leaves.You will make a new slide for each of them. You can use the websites on the "Helpful Links" page to learn what each plant part does.

Things to Remember

  • Each slide should have at least 4 sentences on it.
  • Your slide should say what the plant part is named, and how it helps the plant.
  • Each slide should have a drawing on it of the plant part.
