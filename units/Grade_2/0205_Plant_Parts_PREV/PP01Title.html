Using Text in Frames

Today we are going to add to our Plant Parts Project by using the Text Tool to create a Title Slide.Your Title Slide should have a Title for the project, your name, and class number. Other than that you can design it in any way you want. But you have to use the Pencil Tool. No Clip Art Please:)

If anyone makes a really nice Title Slide that they're really proud of, they can ask me to put their work on this webpage so others can see their awesome work!

If you want your work on the website, please use the form below.

Things to Remember:

  • Your Slide should have your name, class number, and a title.
  • Your Name and the Title of your Show should start with a capital letter.
  • Your Title and the picture in it should be about plants.
  • Please remember to make it awesome.

Extension Activity:

If you finish early, use the Pencil Tool with Duplicate to make a Pattern Border around your title slide
