Getting Started with Powerpoint

Today we are going to start using Powerpoint to make our Plant Parts Slideshow. Let's start our project by opening up a new Slideshow in Powerpoint. Look on the dock for this icon to open it up.

The first thing we need to do is choose a Theme for our slideshow. In Powerpoint Theme means how the slides will look. Once you choose a theme, you will see your title slide. This is the first slide of your slideshow.

For the title - write: Plant Parts. For the subtitle - write your name and class number.
