Teaching Point

Students will learn how to navigate and create slides in Powerpoint.

Adding a New Slide

The next slides you will add will be about The different parts of a plant. Notice how when you add a new slide, you can see all of your other slides on the side of the screen.

Your first slide should be an introduction. A good introduction tells people what they are going to learn about in the Slideshow.

  • Add a new slide, and for the title write Introduction.
  • In the box below you will write 4 sentences about what the slideshow will be about.
  • When you are done with one sentence, press the "Return" key to go down to the next line. There should only be one sentence on every line.

Today's Assignment

Add a new slide to your powerpoint. Give it the title "Introduction". Write at least 4 sentences introducing your topic in the Text Box of the Slide.
