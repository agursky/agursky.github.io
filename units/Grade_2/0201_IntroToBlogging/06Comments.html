How to Comment on your Friends Blog

Every blog entry has a place for comments. That means that when you are commenting on your friends blog, you are NOT leaving a comment for the whole blog, you are leaving a comment for one blog entry.

To Comment on a friends blog:

  1. Go to your friends blog. Remember the blog button is in the top right corner of their page.
  2. Click on the title of the blog entry you want to comment on.
  3. Click on the comments on the side.
  4. Press the plus sign near the comments to add a comment.
  5. After you leave your comment, press the check button near the commments to save it.
