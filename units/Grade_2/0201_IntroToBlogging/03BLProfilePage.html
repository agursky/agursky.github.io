Teaching Point:

Today we will learn how to set up our About Me Page.

What is a Profile Page?

Everyone who has a blog also has an About Me Page. An About Me is a place where you tell people about yourself. That way if someone who doesn't know you clicks on your page, they will know a little bit about you.

Today's Assignment

Write your About Me Page. Some good information to write about would be your name, what grade you are in, and some of your favorite things.
