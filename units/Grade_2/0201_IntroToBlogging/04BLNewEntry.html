Today we will learn how to make a new Blog Entry.

Making a New Blog Entry.

Everytime you have something new to put on your blog, you make a new Blog Entry. Blog Pages have the newest Blog Entries on the top, and then you can scroll down to see older ones. To make a New Blog Entry:

  1. Press the Plus Sign Button on the top.
  2. Choose New Blog Post in my Blog.
  3. It will ask you what you want to name your blog entry. Give it a name that says what the blog entry will show
  4. Press "Add"
  5. Write your blog entry into the big box
  6. Press "Save" button on top
