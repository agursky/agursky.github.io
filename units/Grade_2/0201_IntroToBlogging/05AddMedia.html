How to Add Pictures or Videos to your Blog

When you looked at other students blogs, you saw that their blogs weren't just writing. They were also posts with pictures and videos. We will be showing our work on the blogs and that will mean we will be posting pictures and videos too. Today we will practice posting a picture onto our blogs.

To post a picture or video onto your blog:

  1. First log into your blog and go to your blog page
  2. Start a new blog post by pressing the Plus sign button
  3. When you are in your new blog post, look in the top left corner for a button that looks like a paper clip.
  4. Click on "Choose File"
  5. Find the picture or video you want to add. Usually it will be on your Desktop
  6. Press "Upload"
  7. That's it, you're done. Don't forget to press Save
