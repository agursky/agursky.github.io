Logging into your Blog

Logging into your blog is easy. You go to the class blogs page, and press the little Lock Button near the top. Then you type your username and password.

  • Your username is your name. Make sure you start your first name with a capital letter, and your last name with a capital letter. All of the other letters should be lowercase. For Example: John Smith, Maggie Lee
  • Your password is the letter s. Make sure it is lower case.
  • There should be NO EXTRA SPACES in your username or password. If you put an extra space it won't work.

    • How to Find Your Friends Blogs

    Look for the 3 Lines in the top left corner. That is the button to see everyone's blog. Press that, and in the menu, choose "All People".
