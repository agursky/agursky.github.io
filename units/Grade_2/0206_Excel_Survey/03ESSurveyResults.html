Teaching Point:

Today we are going to collect data about peoples favorite subjects in school.

Survey Results

Remember, a survey gives you information about what people think and puts it together in a neat, easy to read list. That is why Excel is such a good tool to use to make them. Today we will take a class vote and write the results into our Excel Spreadsheet. Each number of favorite votes for a subject in school should go in the cell next to the name of the subject. For example - if art is in cell A5, and 10 people voted for it, we should write "10" in cell B5 - the cell next to A5.

Today's Assignment

Record data into your spreadsheet. Format the information to make it look nice.
