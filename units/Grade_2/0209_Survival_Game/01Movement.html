01: X and Y Numbers

Teaching Point

Students will learn how to make their characters move in Scratch.

X Numbers and Y Numbers

Everything in scratch has 2 numbers attached to it that tell us where the sprite is. The 1st number is the X Number. The 2nd number is the Y Number. The X Number tells us how far to the left or right a sprite is. The Y Number tells us how far up or down it is.

We can control where the sprite goes by changing it's X number (to make it go left or right) and it's Y number (to make it go up or down). The codes to do this are in the blue menu.

What do you think the difference is between 'setting' the x and y numbers and 'changing' them?
