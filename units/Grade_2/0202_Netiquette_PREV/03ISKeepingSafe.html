Staying Safe on the Internet

Hectors World Internet Safety Video

The Internet is a place where anyone can go, and anyone can post information. This means that a lot of really cool people post a lot of really cool stuff, but it also means that there are bad people there too. We have no way to know who is good and who is bad. To stay safe on the Internet it is important that we:

  • Never talk to strangers we meet online.
  • NEVER agree to meet anyone you meet online in real life.
  • Keep your information private. You don't need to post your name, phone number, address, where you go to school, or anything else about you on the internet.
