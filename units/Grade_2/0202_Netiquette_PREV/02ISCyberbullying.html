Teaching Point:

Today we will learn what Cyberbullying is.

Don't be an Online Bully

Hector's World Cyberbullying Video

Cyberbullying is just like regular bullying, except that it happens on the computer. Cyberbullying is when someon is mean to you on the computer for no reason, and when they keep doing it even when you ask them to stop. They may say mean things to you, or about you to other people. Cyberbullying can be:

  • Telling others not to be friends with you
  • Saying mean things to you in comments or on your Social Network
  • Posting pictures of you or other information about you without your permission

If someone ever Cyberbullies you, the best thing to do is tell an adult right away.
