Working with Pages in Google Sites

A website is made of many pages. As we've seen, on a good website, the topics of the different pages make sense, and are all part of the main topic. Every circle in your site map will be a different page.

To make a new page, press the button that looks like a page with a plus sign on it at the top of the screen. It will ask you for the name of your page. For Template, choose "Web Page", and for location choose "Top Level". Once you are done, press "Create".

If you are curious about the other choices - for Template:

  • Announcements creates a page that works like a blog. You can add announcements to the top of it.
  • File Cabinet is a page that can store files that other people can download from your website.
  • List is a....list.

Today's Assignment

Make one page for each circle in your Site Map. The names on the Site Map should match the names of your pages.

Extension Activity

Make an extra page that uses the "List" Template. You can use it to show links to other good websites about your topic.
