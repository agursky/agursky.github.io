Getting Organized

A good website is a site that is so easy to figure out that no one needs to teach it to you. You know where to go, because the way that it is organized makes sense. If a website is organized well, you never really think about it.

Good organization means the links to the pages in your website are easy to find, and they cover topics in a way that makes it easy for the user to learn about the page. Take a look at some of the pages below. Notice how they divide the topics they are about into different subtopics.

Now you will go find your own websites to evaluate. You can type in the address of a website you know, or use Google. Please use the form below to share your findings.
