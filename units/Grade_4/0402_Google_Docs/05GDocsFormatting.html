Formatting in Google Docs

Formatting means how the file looks. This can mean where the words are on the page, how big the letters are, or anything else that has to do with how things look in your file. In Google Docs, the Formatting controls are in the web browser window. You can change the letter shape (that's called font), the letter size, and the letter color.

Today's Assignment

Today you will format your Google Docs Document.

    Formatting Checklist
  • Your title should be "What is Google Docs?". It should be at the top of the page
  • Your title should be Bold.
  • Your title should be size 18
  • You should skip two lines before you start your paragraph. (That means press return twice).
  • Your paragraph should be size 14.
  • It should not be Bold.
  • The writing on your page can be any letter shape (font) and any color you like.
