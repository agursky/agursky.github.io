Today we will learn how to share our work on Google Docs.

Sharing Your Google Docs Files

Sharing your work is easy. The quickest way to share your work with another Google Drive user is to click on the file, and click on the big blue "Share" button in the top right corner. This will open a window that asks you who to share with. Just type the username of whomever you want to share with into the box. Don't forget the Q in ps169q.org.
