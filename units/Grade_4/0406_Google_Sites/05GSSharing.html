Teaching Point

Today we will review how to Copy and Paste, and use that skill to share our Google Sites on Google Classroom.

Sharing your Google Site

We are going to share our Google Site the same way we shared our Google Form. We will copy and paste the web address of our Google Site into Google Classroom, so that our friends can see our work. To do this we have to go to our Google Site and:

  • Highlight the web address.
  • Go to Edit-Copy to copy it.
  • Go to Google Classroom.
  • Use Edit-Paste to paste the web address of our Google Site as a Comment in the Google Sites thread.

Today's Assignment

Share your Google Site by copying and pasting the web address as a comment in Google Classroom.
