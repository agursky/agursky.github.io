Teaching Point

Today we will learn how to make and change different webpages on Google Sites.

Setting up your Website

To begin, we will make our website and then make each of the different pages we will have on the site. We will do our writing and add pictures later. First we need to make the site by going to sites.google.com.

Next we will make the pages we will need for our site. We will use our Sitemap the help us. Each little circle in our Sitemap will be it's own page.

Today's Assignment

Start by making your webpage. After that, start making the pages. Make one page for each circle in your Site Map. The names on the Site Map should match the names of your pages.

Extension Activity

Make an extra page that uses the "List" Template. You can use it to show links to other good websites about your topic.
