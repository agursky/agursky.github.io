Transitions and Animations

We've already used Transitions and Animations in our Slideshows on Powerpoint. We use Transitions to move between slides, and animations to make the slides fun once they get there. Doing special effects is easy in Google Slides.

In Google Slides, these two special effects are in the same place. Just press the Transitions Button on the Menu bar.

  1. To add a Transition - in the Transitions Menu, click where it says NO TRANSITION to choose a new transition.
  2. To add an Animation - choose what you want to animate by clicking on it with the mouse.
  3. Then in the Transitions Menu - click Add Animation and choose an animation from the menu.
