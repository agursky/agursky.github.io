Teaching Point:

Today we will learn how to change the Layout of our Slides.

What is Layout?

The word Layout means how things are organized to look neat. When we want to write in a Slideshow, the Layout is very simple, but when we want to add something else, like a picture we need to make sure there is room for it by changing the layout. To change the layout and add a picture:

  1. Look for the Layout Button on the menu bar.
  2. Title with two columns is the best choice for writing with a picture.
  3. Drag a picture from one of the research websites onto your desktop.
  4. Drag the picture from your Desktop to your slide.
  5. In the Slides, drag the pictures corners to make it bigger or smaller if you need to.
