Safe Navigation Online

There are a lot of different situations that we might find ourselves in when we are online, and it is not always clear what the right thing to do is. That is why it is important to ask an adult when we feel unsure. We should also make sure that our parents or gaurdians know what sites we are using, and we should always aks first if we ever need to use a site that asks for personal information.
