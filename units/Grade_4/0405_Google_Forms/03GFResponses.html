Seeing our Results

What is really cool about Google Forms is how the results work. When someone fills out a Google Form and presses Submit, their answers go into a spreadsheet which is saved automatically in our Google Drive. We can vew all of the answers, and even sort them if we need to. To see your results:

  • Go to your Google Form
  • Click VIEW RESPONSES on the top.
  • In the responses page, click VIEW - SUMMARY OF RESPONSES to have your responses turned into graphs and other easy to read data.
