Teaching Point:

Today we will learn how to make The letters small or big.

Changing the Letter Size

Just like we can change the color of the letters, we can also change the sizes. We have a lot of different sizes to choose from, and changing them is easy.

To change the size of the letters, we have to highlight the letters we want to change. Once they are highlighted, we can look for the numbers next to the letter and change it. A big number makes the letters bigger, a small number makes them small.

Today's Assignment

Highlight the words next to the animal pictures, and make the big animal names have big letters and the small animal names have small ones.

Extension Activity

Write some of your favorite animal names and change the word sizes so they match if the animal is big or small.
