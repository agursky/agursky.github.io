Today we will learn how to make our pictures move in Frames.

Making our Pictures Move

In Frames we can make our pictures move around, just like cartoons. The way that we do this is we make a copy of our pictures, and then we move some of the things in the copy to different parts of the screen. If we watch the first copy and the second copy together really quickly, it looks like the pictures are moving. The key to doing this the right way is to not move anything too far from where it started. If the pictures only move a short bit, it looks like a cartoon. To make your picture move:

  • Click on the box that is your picture at the bottom of the page.
  • Click on the red DUPLICATE button.
  • In the new copy, move some of the small stamps around. Don't move the text.
  • Press the play button at the bottom of the screen.

Today's Assignment

Use the Duplicate button to make a copy of your seasons picture. Then make the picture move.
