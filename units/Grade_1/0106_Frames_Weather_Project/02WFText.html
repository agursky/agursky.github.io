Teaching Point:

Today we will learn how to write in Frames.

Writing in Frames

Writing in Frames is just like writing in Kid Pix. The only difference is that instead of clicking a "T TOOL", we click an "A TOOL". After that, we get a box to type in.

It is easy to change the way that the letters look in Frames. Just highlight the letters, and use the tools on the side. You can change the font, the letter color, and the letter size - just like in Kid Pix.

Today's Assignment

Add writing to your winter slide. Start with "I like winter because" and then write 2 or 3 things that you really like about winter. Do this for each season.

Extension Activity

Change your letter sizes, colors, and fonts to make your Slideshow look more fun.
