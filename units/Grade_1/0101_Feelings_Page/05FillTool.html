Today we will learn how to use the Fill Tool to color things in quickly.

The Fill Tool

One of the best things about drawing on the computer is that it is easy to make changes to our drawings after we are done. With crayons or markers, you can't chane the colors once you are done with your drawing. With a computer you can. To change colors we use the Fill Tool.

With the Fill Tool we just choose a color, and click on a part of our drawing. Whatever part we clicked on will change to the color we chose.
