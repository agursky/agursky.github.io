Animation in Frames

We practiced using Animation in Frames for our last project. Remember, we can make our pictures move by making copies of our picture, and changing each picture just a little bit. To do this:

  • Click on your picture on the side menu.
  • Press COPY on top.
  • Press PASTE on top.
  • Change the copy of your picture just a little.
  • Repeat all of the steps as many times as you need.

Today's Assignment

Use COPY and PASTE to make your picture move. Have your character walk from one side of the picture to the other.

Extension Activity

Add talking to your animation using comic book speech bubbles.
