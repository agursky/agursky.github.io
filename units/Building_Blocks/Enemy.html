01: X and Y Numbers

Teaching Point

Students will learn use "Point Towards" to make an enemy in their game.

Making an enemy

Enemies make the game exciting. We can make enemies chase us by using the "Point Toward" block with some other blocks that we know.

When we use "Move 10 Steps", it makes the sprite go forward. To make an enemy chase us, we just need to make sure it's always facing us. Then wherever we go - move 10 steps will make the enemy go to us.

How can we combine these blocks to make an enemy face us and move toward us? Is there anything else we should add?

Today's Assignment

Make an enemy to chase you in the game.

Extra Credit

Use an If block with a Touching block to make your sprite change color or change to a 'hurt' costume when the enemy touches the hero.
