Smart Folders

Smart Folders are special folders that search the computer. You tell the folder what to look for and the folder will fill itself up with all of those things. You may make a Smart Folder to fill itself with everything you made in the last 2 weeks, or all of your Frames files, or all of your videos. Anything you want to find, you can find with a smart folder. To Make a Smart Folder:

  1. Make sure you are in FINDER.
  2. Click on File - New Smart Folder
  3. IF YOU WANT:Use the Pluse Sign Button to add Search ideas.
  4. IF YOU WANT:Write the name of the thing you are looking for in the box in the corner.
