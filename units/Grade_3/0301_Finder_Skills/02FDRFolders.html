Teaching Point

Today we will learn how make our own Folders in Finder.

Making Our Own Folders

It is easy to make your own folder in finder. Open a Finder Window and click on the "Gears" Button. Then choose "New Folder". This will make a new Folder called "Untitled Folder". You can give it a name right away, or change it's name later by doing "click, pause, click" on the Name of the Folder.

Today's Assignment

Today we will make at least 2 new folders on the Desktop. Make one folder for "Old Work" and put all of your work from last year into it. Make another folder for Pictures, and put any pictures you have on your Desktop in it.

Extension Activity

Make more folders to make your Desktop more organized.
