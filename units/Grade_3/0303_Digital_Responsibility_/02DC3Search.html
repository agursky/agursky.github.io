Safe Places Online

Are you allowed to walk anywhere you want outside? Why or why not? How about online? What kinds of places are you allowed to go WITHOUT your parents? What about online?

When we are looking for information online, many times our first idea is to go to Google, but when we do that we will see A LOT of results, and most of them are not written for children. This may mean that the words and the language are too hard for students in elementary school to understand. That is why when we do something in computer class that involves research, I usually pick out the sites for you and post them onto Portaportal or one of these project webpages.

If you need to do research on your own, there are search engines that are built just for kids. Here are a few really good ones.
