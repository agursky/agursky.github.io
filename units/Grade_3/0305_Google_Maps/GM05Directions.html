What we are going to learn today

Today we are going to learn how to get directions on Google Maps.

Getting Directions

One of the most important ways we use Google Maps is to get directions. We can use it to get directions from any place in the world to any other place. There are 3 types of directions Google Maps can give us:

  • Driving
  • Buses, Trains
  • Walking

    • As you can see, Google Maps Directions gives us a lot of information. It tells us:

  • Where to go and when to turn.
  • How long it might take to get there.
  • If there is traffic.

    • You can even choose different routes to take, based on which is the quickest.
