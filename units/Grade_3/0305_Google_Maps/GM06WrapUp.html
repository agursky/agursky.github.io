Project Wrap Up

Today we are going to review what we have done, and make sure that we have all of our screenshots.

Summary

You should each have a folder on your Desktop named "Google Maps Screenshot Folder". This folder should have the following things in it:

  • 3 Screenshots of your country from Satellite View, named Screenshot 1, Screenshot 2, and Screenshot 3
  • 3 Screenshots of your country from Street View, named Street View 1, Street View 2, and Street View 3
  • 2 Screenshots of your country from "Photospheres", named PS1, PS2
  • 2 Screenshots of your country from "Directions", named Directions 1, Directions 2

    • Today's Task

    Please take a screenshot of your Folder and post it to your blog. If you are missing Map screeshots, or your Map screenshots are not named correctly, fix this first, then post to your blog.
