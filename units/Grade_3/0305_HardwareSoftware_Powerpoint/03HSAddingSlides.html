Adding a New Slide

The next slides you will add will be about Hardware and Software. Notice how when you add a new slide, you can see all of your other slides on the side of the screen.

Your first slide should be an introduction. A good introduction tells people what they are going to learn about in the Slideshow.

Add a new slide, and for the title write Introduction.

In the box below you will write 4 sentences about Hardware and Software. When you are done with one sentence, press the "Return" key to go down to the next line. There should only be one sentence on every line.

Today's Assignment

Add a new slide to your powerpoint. Give it the title "Introduction". Write at least 4 sentences introducing your topic in the Text Box of the Slide.
