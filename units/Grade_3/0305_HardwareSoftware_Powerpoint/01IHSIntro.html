Teaching Point

Students will learn what Hardware is, and what Software is.

What is Hardware? What is Software?

Whenever we use a computer (or a phone, or a tablet) we are using Hardware and Software. Hardware means the parts of the computer that we can touch with our hands. Software means the apps on the computer.

Hardware helps us communicate with the computer.
