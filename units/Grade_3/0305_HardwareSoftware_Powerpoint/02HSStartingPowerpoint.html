Getting Started with Powerpoint

For this project we will be using a new app called Microsoft Powerpoint. Powerpoint is used for making slideshow. Look for the icon below to open it up.

The first thing we need to do is choose a Theme for our slideshow. In Powerpoint Theme means how the slides will look. Once you choose a theme, you will see your title slide. This is the first slide of your slideshow.

For the title - write: Hardware and Software. For the subtitle - write your name and class number.
