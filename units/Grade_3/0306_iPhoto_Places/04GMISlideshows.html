Today we will learn how make Around the World Slideshows in iPhoto.

Slideshows in iPhoto

It is easy to make a Slideshow in iPhoto. You just have to pick which pictures you want to show, and then press a button. We can make slideshows about anything we want and even choose music and other effects to go with it. We are going to make an around the world slideshow. The slideshow will show a map of the world, and use the information about where each picture was taken to show the pictures in the places that they belong on the map. To make a slideshow in iPhoto:

  • Drag the pictures you want over to the sidebar to make an Album of them.
  • In the Album drag the pictures into the order you want to show them.
  • Press the Slideshow button on the bottom of the iPhoto Window.
  • When the slideshow plays, press the buttons on the bottom to add effects to it - the most important button for this project is Themes - Choose the Places Theme to give your slideshow a map.

Today's Assignment

Put your pictures together into an album, and choose the Places Theme to turn them into an Around The World Slideshow.

Extension Activity

Click on the gear button on the bottom of the screen to add other effects.
