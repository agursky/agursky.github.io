Special Effects

The most fun part of using Powerpoint is adding Special Effects to make your slideshow cooler and more fun to watch. Add these Special Effects to each of your slides.

Transitions: Special Effect for moving from one slide to the next.

Animation: Changes how the words and pictures come onto your slide.
